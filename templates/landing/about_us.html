Our Journey

From a small startup to India's leading lead generation platform

2020

The Beginning

OptimizedLeads was founded with a simple mission: to help businesses find their ideal customers. Started with a team of 3 passionate individuals in Mumbai.

2021

First Milestone

Reached 500+ happy clients and generated over 100,000 leads. Expanded our services to include Real Estate and Study Abroad sectors.

2022

Rapid Growth

Crossed 2,000 clients and 1 million leads generated. Launched Education and Job Consultancy verticals. Team grew to 25+ professionals.

2023

Innovation Year

Launched our advanced lead scoring system and CRM integration. Achieved 85% average conversion rate across all industries.

2024

Market Leadership

Became India's #1 lead generation platform with 5,000+ clients and 2.5M+ leads generated. Expanding to new cities and industries.
